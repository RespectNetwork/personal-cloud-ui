Why Personal Cloud??

Personal cloud is the individuals collection of digital content, services and apps which are seamlessly accessible across any device.The personal cloud is not a tangible entity, but rather the realization of four different types of experience in which users store, synchronize,stream and share content on a contextual basis, moving from one platform, screen and location to another.Founded on interconnected services and applications, it both reflects and sets consumers expectations for how next-generation computing services will work.
